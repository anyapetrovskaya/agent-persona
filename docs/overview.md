# Agent Persona — How It Works

This guide explains what Agent Persona does and how to get the most out of it. For quick setup, see the [main README](../README.md).

---

## What Is It?

Agent Persona gives your AI partner persistent memory and an adaptable personality. Without it, every new chat starts from zero. With it, your AI partner remembers your conversations, preferences, decisions, and projects — so each session picks up where the last one left off.

Over time, your AI partner gets better at working with you. It learns your communication style, tracks your tasks, and builds up knowledge about what matters to you.

## What It Remembers

Your AI partner maintains three layers of memory:

- **Recent conversations** — exact recall of what was said in the last few sessions
- **Session summaries** — highlights from each session: what was discussed, what was decided, what's pending
- **Long-term knowledge** — durable facts about you, your projects, and your preferences, distilled over time

Memory builds up naturally as you work. At the end of each day, your AI partner consolidates what it learned into lasting knowledge.

## What You Need to Do

Almost everything happens automatically. The only thing to remember: say good night to your partner when you're done for the day. That's when they process and consolidate everything from the session — think of it as letting them sleep on it.

## Forgetting

Memory isn't just about remembering — things that aren't used naturally fade over time, keeping your AI partner focused on what's relevant.

You're always in control. Ask your partner what's fading, and they'll show you. Tell them to remember something important, and they'll pin it. Tell them to forget something, and they will — but nothing is ever permanently gone. You can always ask them to bring it back.

## Conversations & Living Documents

**Conversation threads** let you maintain separate discussions on different topics. Each thread has its own context, so switching between topics doesn't cause confusion.

**Living documents** are reference docs that evolve over time — the "current truth" on a topic you revisit. Think of them as the document a conversation produces, separate from the discussion itself.

If a conversation and a living document share a name, loading one automatically loads both.
