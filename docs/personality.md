# Personality

Agent Persona adapts its communication style to match how you like to work. This page explains how.

---

## Changing Style

Tell your AI partner what you want in natural language:

- `be more concise` — shorter, more direct responses
- `be more supportive` — warmer, more encouraging tone
- `be more critical` — challenges your assumptions, gives direct feedback
- `be brief but funny` — describe any combination you want
- `back to normal` — reset to default

Changes take effect immediately and persist for the session. Over time, your AI partner also learns your preferences from how you respond — if you consistently prefer shorter answers, it trends that way on its own.

## What Gets Adjusted

Your AI partner has several style dimensions it can tune:

| Trait | Low end | High end |
|-------|---------|----------|
| **Warmth** | Neutral, businesslike | Friendly, personable |
| **Verbosity** | Terse, just the answer | Detailed, explanatory |
| **Humor** | Serious, straightforward | Dry wit, playful |
| **Criticality** | Gentle suggestions | Direct challenges |
| **Encouragement** | Matter-of-fact | Supportive, affirming |

## Presets

There are named style profiles you can switch to:

| Preset | Style |
|--------|-------|
| `expert-laconic` | Brief, direct, dry humor |
| `concise-unbiased` | Brief, neutral, balanced |
| `bubbly-chatty` | Warm, verbose, encouraging |
| `critic` | High criticality, direct feedback |
| `supportive` | High warmth, high encouragement |

Just say the name or describe what you want — your AI partner figures out the rest.

## Learned Rules

As you work together, your AI partner may notice patterns and suggest behavioral rules — things like "always explain trade-offs" or "prefer library defaults over custom implementations." You approve or reject these suggestions, and approved rules become part of how the agent operates going forward.

These are explicit, user-verified rules — your AI partner won't change its behavior without your approval.

## How It Evolves

- **Immediate** — "be more concise" changes the style right now
- **Session-level** — style persists for the current session
- **Long-term** — your AI partner gradually adjusts its baseline based on what resonates with you over many sessions

The result: an AI partner that starts generic and becomes increasingly *yours* over time.
